Welcome to Nexura's 3D Animation and Modelling Department:

The 3D Animation Department is a creative hub where art meets technology to bring imagination to life. Specializing in the creation of stunning visuals, lifelike characters, and immersive worlds, this department plays a vital role in industries such as entertainment, gaming, advertising, education, and beyond

Equipped with cutting-edge tools and software, the department fosters innovation and technical excellence. Students and professionals here master skills like 3D modeling, rigging, texturing, lighting, animation, and rendering while gaining hands-on experience in crafting compelling stories and realistic simulations

Driven by creativity and precision, the 3D Animation Department bridges artistic vision and technical expertise, producing work that captivates audiences and pushes the boundaries of visual storytelling.
